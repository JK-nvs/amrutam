Amrutam Panchkarma Ayurvedic Hospital is a sanctuary of holistic healing nestled in the heart of Navsari, Gujarat. Founded in 2014 by Dr. Nitiksha Solanki, our institution has remained steadfast in its commitment to authentic, classical Ayurveda — blending time-honoured Panchkarma protocols with genuine compassion for every patient we serve.

Our approach is deeply personal. Before any treatment begins, our senior Vaidyas conduct thorough Prakriti analysis to understand your unique constitution — because true Ayurvedic healing is never one-size-fits-all.
